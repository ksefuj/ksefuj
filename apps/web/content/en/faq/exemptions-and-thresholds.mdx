## Thresholds and Exemptions

### Who is exempt from KSeF?

Exemptions include taxpayers without a registered office or fixed place of business in Poland, invoices issued under special procedures (OSS/IOSS), and invoices from cash registers. The full list of exemptions is defined in the Polish VAT Act — check ksef.podatki.gov.pl for details.

### What about VAT-exempt businesses?

VAT-exempt entities must use KSeF from January 1, 2027. If you issue invoices documenting exempt activities, you have more time to prepare. Consult your accountant to determine exactly when the obligation applies to you.
