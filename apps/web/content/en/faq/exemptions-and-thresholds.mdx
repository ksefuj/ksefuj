## Thresholds and Exemptions

<Question q="What is the 10,000 PLN threshold?">
  The original KSeF legislation included a provision allowing taxpayers with monthly sales not
  exceeding 10,000 PLN to use an extended implementation deadline. These rules were amended several
  times — check the current regulations at ksef.podatki.gov.pl or consult your accountant.
  Regardless of the threshold, it is worth preparing for KSeF as early as possible.
</Question>

<Question q="How is the 10,000 PLN threshold calculated?">
  The threshold is calculated as the total value of sales in a given month, not the number of
  invoices issued. Both VAT-taxable and VAT-exempt sales count. If you are approaching the threshold
  or are unsure how the rules apply to you, consult your accountant — the regulations in this area
  have been amended.
</Question>

<Question q="When does KSeF apply to me if I'm below the threshold?">
  The date from which KSeF applies to smaller taxpayers depends on VAT status and current
  regulations — the rules have been amended several times. Always verify the applicable dates
  directly at ksef.podatki.gov.pl or consult your accountant.
</Question>

<Question q="Does a flat-rate farmer need to use KSeF?">
  Flat-rate farmers use a special tax regime and are generally exempt from the KSeF obligation. RR
  invoices (issued by the buyer to the flat-rate farmer) are subject to separate regulations. If you
  also run other VAT-taxable activities, consult your accountant.
</Question>

<Question q="Who is exempt from KSeF?">
  Exemptions include taxpayers without a registered office or fixed place of business in Poland,
  invoices issued under special procedures (OSS/IOSS), and invoices from cash registers. The full
  list of exemptions is defined in the Polish VAT Act and secondary legislation — check
  ksef.podatki.gov.pl for details.
</Question>

<Question q="Does a foundation or association need to use KSeF?">
  It depends on whether the organization is registered as an active VAT taxpayer. If so — from April
  1, 2026 it must use KSeF for its VAT invoices. Organizations conducting exclusively VAT-exempt
  activities join the system from January 1, 2027. Organizations that do not issue VAT invoices at
  all are not subject to the obligation.
</Question>
