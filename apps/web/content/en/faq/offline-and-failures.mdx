## Offline Mode and Failures

<Question q="What should I do if KSeF is down?">
  When KSeF is unavailable, the Ministry of Finance officially announces emergency mode. During this
  time you can issue invoices outside the system with appropriate markings. After KSeF is restored,
  you have a set time to submit the pending invoices. Check status at ksef.podatki.gov.pl.
</Question>

<Question q="What is offline mode?">
  Offline mode allows you to issue invoices outside KSeF when you have no internet access or the
  system is unavailable. An offline invoice gets a special QR code downloaded in advance from MF and
  must be submitted to KSeF once connectivity is restored. You can use offline mode proactively, not
  just during failures.
</Question>

<Question q="What is emergency mode?">
  Emergency mode is officially declared by the Ministry of Finance when KSeF is unavailable due to
  technical or administrative reasons. During this time the obligation to send invoices to KSeF is
  suspended — you issue invoices outside the system and commit to uploading them after the outage.
  Status updates appear on ksef.podatki.gov.pl.
</Question>

<Question q="How long do I have to submit invoices after an outage?">
  After emergency mode ends, you generally have until the next business day to submit invoices
  issued in offline or emergency mode. The exact deadline depends on the length of the outage and
  may be announced by the MF along with the system restoration notice. We recommend sending pending
  invoices as soon as possible.
</Question>

<Question q="What does the QR code in offline mode look like?">
  The QR code on an offline invoice is a special token downloaded in advance from the KSeF system —
  each business can download a pool of codes in case of connectivity loss. The code identifies the
  invoice and enables its later association with KSeF after the document is uploaded. Without a
  valid QR code, an offline invoice may be challenged.
</Question>

<Question q="How do I know if KSeF is unavailable?">
  The official KSeF system status is published at ksef.podatki.gov.pl. Your invoicing software
  should also display a notification when it cannot connect to the MF API. Planned outages are
  usually announced in advance; unplanned ones — immediately.
</Question>
