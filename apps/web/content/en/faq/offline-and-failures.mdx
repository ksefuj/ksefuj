## Offline Mode and Failures

### What should I do if KSeF is down?

When KSeF is unavailable, the Ministry of Finance officially announces emergency mode. During this time you can issue invoices outside the system with appropriate markings. After KSeF is restored, you have a set time to submit the pending invoices. Check status at ksef.podatki.gov.pl.

### What is offline mode?

Offline mode allows you to issue invoices outside KSeF when you have no internet access or the system is unavailable. An offline invoice gets a special QR code downloaded in advance from MF and must be submitted to KSeF once connectivity is restored. You can use offline mode proactively, not just during failures.

### What is emergency mode?

Emergency mode is officially declared by the Ministry of Finance when KSeF is unavailable due to technical or administrative reasons. During this time the obligation to send invoices to KSeF is suspended — you issue invoices outside the system and commit to uploading them after the outage. Status updates appear on ksef.podatki.gov.pl.
