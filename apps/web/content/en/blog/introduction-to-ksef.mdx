KSeF (**Krajowy System e-Faktur** — National e-Invoice System) is Poland's mandatory platform for
issuing and receiving invoices in a structured XML format. Starting **April 1, 2026**, all VAT
taxpayers in Poland must use KSeF.

## What this means in practice

If you run a business registered for VAT in Poland, your invoices must:

1. Be issued in XML format conforming to the FA(3) schema
2. Be submitted to KSeF before or at the time of delivery to the recipient
3. Include a KSeF number as confirmation of acceptance

<Info>
  Paper invoices and PDFs **will not have legal standing** once KSeF becomes mandatory for your
  taxpayer category.
</Info>

## How ksefuj.to helps

**ksefuj.to** lets you validate XML invoices before submitting them to KSeF — entirely in your
browser, with no data sent to any server.
